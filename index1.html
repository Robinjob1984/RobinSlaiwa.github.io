PARIS

The City of Light


Unquestionably, one of the most fascinating and stunning cities on the planet is Paris. Paris will capture your heart and soul with its opulent structures, stylish cafés, and many art galleries. Make sure to balance your time between being the ultimate tourist, scaling the Eiffel Tower, and blending in with the locals. Get a Pastis and join the locals in one of the city's many pubs for a genuine sense of daily life in the French capital.

Top 5 attractions in Paris:

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Louvre Museum
  • Cathédrale Notre-Dame de Paris
  • Arc de Triomphe
  • Palace of Versailles
